About

Dr. Tapash Das is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Education at Kazi Nazrul University, West Bengal. He earned his Ph.D. in Education from the University of Kalyani in 2023. His academic interests focus on equity, access, and quality in education with special emphasis on teacher education, STEM education, artificial intelligence in education, and education for disadvantaged groups.
